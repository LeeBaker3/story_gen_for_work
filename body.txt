## Summary
Wizard step pills are rendered as clickable div elements. They are not naturally focusable, do not expose button semantics, and rely on mouse click handlers. Keyboard and screen-reader users cannot navigate wizard steps.

## Files Affected
- frontend/index.html ~line 74
- frontend/script.js ~line 233

## Recommendation
Render the stepper as an ordered list of button elements. Keep aria-current on the active step. Enforce enabled/disabled semantics for reachable steps.

## Acceptance Criteria
- All wizard steps can be reached and activated via Tab + Enter
- Screen reader announces step name and current state
