# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-13

This policy describes how Story Generator handles information when you use the current application.

## What We Collect

We may collect and store:
- account details such as username, email address, password hash, and role;
- story inputs such as titles, outlines, character details, and editor changes;
- uploaded character images and other files you submit;
- generated story text, generated images, PDFs, and related metadata;
- operational data such as logs, error reports, and monitoring information;
- request metadata needed to run and secure the service.

## How We Use Information

We use this information to:
- create and manage accounts;
- generate, edit, preview, export, and store stories;
- generate and regenerate character reference images;
- moderate content and administer the service;
- diagnose errors, abuse, and reliability issues;
- maintain logs, metrics, and backups for normal operations.

## Sharing And Processors

We may share data with service providers that help us operate the product, including:
- OpenAI for story and image generation;
- hosting, storage, and logging providers used to run the application;
- third-party tools required to secure or observe the service.

The current known provider list is documented in [Subprocessors And Service Providers](subprocessors.md).

We do not sell personal information in this repository baseline.

## Storage And Retention

The current application stores data locally by default in the repository-configured data directory and database.
Content remains stored until it is deleted, replaced, or removed during routine maintenance or account handling.
Some operational logs may be retained longer when needed for security, debugging, or legal obligations.

For the current retention categories and deletion/export handling, see [Privacy Operations](privacy-operations.md).

## Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, but no system is perfectly secure.
Auth tokens are currently stored in the browser by the frontend, so users should treat their device and browser profile as sensitive.

## Your Choices

Where supported by the running product or by manual support handling, you may request help with account access, content removal, or other data questions through the support process in this pack.

Short-form AI processing language for product surfaces is available in [AI Processing Disclosure](ai-processing-disclosure.md).

## Changes

This policy may be updated as the product and its commercial controls evolve.
