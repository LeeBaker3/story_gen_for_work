# Copyright And IP Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-13

This policy explains how Story Generator handles user inputs, generated output, and rights-related complaints in the current product baseline.

## User Inputs

You keep the rights you already have in the prompts, outlines, story ideas, and other content you submit.
You must have permission to upload character photos or other third-party materials.

## Generated Output

Story Generator does not claim ownership of your original prompt text.
Generated stories, images, and PDFs may still contain or resemble ideas, names, settings, or visual elements that are protected by third-party rights.
You are responsible for reviewing the output before you publish, distribute, or commercially use it.

## No Permission To Infringe

Do not use the service to create or distribute content that knowingly infringes another party's copyright, trademark, or publicity rights.
Do not request the system to reproduce a copyrighted work, logo, or character in a way that violates the rights of the owner.

## Complaints And Takedown Requests

If you believe content in the service infringes your rights, report it through the support process in this pack and identify the content, the right you believe is affected, and the reason you are reporting it.
We may remove or restrict content while we review the report.

## Commercial Scope

This is a first-pass policy for the repository baseline.
If the product introduces paid plans, resale rights, or explicit output licensing later, this document should be updated before launch.
