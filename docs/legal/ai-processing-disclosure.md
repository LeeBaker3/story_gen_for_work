# AI Processing Disclosure

Effective date: 2026-05-13

This short-form disclosure is used by current frontend and support surfaces.
It reflects the current repository baseline and should stay aligned with the
actual AI features that are enabled in the running app.

## Short Form

Story Generator sends the story prompts, character details, uploaded image
inputs, and related content you provide to AI processing services to generate
and refine story text and images.

Generated text, images, and related metadata may be stored in the app so you
can preview, edit, export, moderate, or manage the resulting story.

Do not submit secrets, highly sensitive personal data, or content you do not
have the right to use.

## Reference Copy For Product Surfaces

You are using AI-assisted generation. The content you enter may be sent to
third-party AI processors so the app can create story text, page art, and
character imagery. The app may keep the prompts and generated output as part of
the story record.

## Notes

This disclosure does not promise that a specific provider will always be used,
because the app can be configured for OpenAI or OpenAI-compatible endpoints.
If the product later adds a different processor, this document should be
updated before release.
