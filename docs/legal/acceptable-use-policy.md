# Acceptable Use / Content Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-13

Story Generator may not be used to submit, generate, store, or distribute content that:

- is unlawful, fraudulent, or deceptive;
- harasses, bullies, threatens, or encourages violence;
- is hateful or discriminatory against protected groups;
- contains sexual exploitation, especially involving minors;
- includes malware, credential theft, phishing, or other security abuse;
- violates privacy, confidentiality, or data protection obligations;
- infringes copyright, trademark, publicity, or other IP rights;
- includes uploads you do not have the right to use;
- attempts to bypass moderation, rate limits, access controls, or other protections;
- is used to impersonate a person or organization in a misleading way.

## Story And Image Requests

When using story prompts or character uploads, do not submit content that you are not permitted to process or publish.
Do not use the product to create harmful outputs that target real people or attempt to disguise harmful intent.

## Enforcement

We may review, remove, limit, or suspend content or accounts that violate this policy or create risk to the service or other users.
Repeated abuse may result in longer-term restriction.
